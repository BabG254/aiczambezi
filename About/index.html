Our Heritage

Our History

AIC Zion – Zambezi was established in 2010 as part of the African Inland Church denomination's expansion into the Kiambu County region. What started as a small fellowship of 20 faithful believers has grown into a vibrant community of over 500 members.

The church was founded on the principles of holistic ministry – addressing the spiritual, social, and economic needs of the community. Under the leadership of our founding pastor, Rev. John Kamau, and through God's grace, we have established strong roots in Zambezi, serving as a beacon of hope for the surrounding areas near Limuru.

Today, we continue to honor our heritage while embracing new methods to reach the current generation with the unchanging message of the Gospel.

Our Mission

To preach the Gospel of Jesus Christ, nurture believers in their faith, disciple followers into mature Christians, and impact the community with the love of Christ through compassionate service and evangelism.

Our Vision

To be a transformed church that transforms lives, communities, and nations through the power of the Gospel, raising a generation of committed followers of Christ who impact every sphere of society.
