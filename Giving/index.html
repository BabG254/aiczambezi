Kingdom Investment

Why We Give

At AIC Zion – Zambezi, giving is an act of worship and faith. When you give, you are not merely supporting a church — you are investing in lives being transformed by the Gospel.

Your contributions enable us to run vibrant ministries, reach the unreached, serve the vulnerable, and equip the next generation with sound biblical teaching.

Every shilling given in faith, no matter the amount, goes further than you can imagine when God multiplies it for His glory.

Questions & Answers

Giving FAQs

Is my donation tax deductible?

As a registered faith organization in Kenya, we are able to issue giving records upon request. Please contact us for details specific to your situation.

Can I give towards a specific project?

Yes! You can designate gifts towards specific projects such as our building fund, youth ministry, or missions. Simply mention the project when giving or email us at info@aiczionzambezi.org.

How are donations used?

All offerings are managed by our church treasurer and elder board, with full accountability. Funds go towards pastoral ministry, outreach, church operations, and community programs.

Can I give as a recurring monthly donor?

Absolutely. You can set up a standing order through your bank, or use M-Pesa on the same date each month. Consistent givers powerfully sustain our ministry year-round.

Have Questions About Giving?

We'd love to talk with you. Reach out to our church office or speak to a pastor after service.
