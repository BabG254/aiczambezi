About Our Church

A Place of Worship,
Love & Community

AIC Zion – Zambezi is a Christ-centered church located in Zambezi, Kiambu County near Limuru, Kenya. Our mission is to spread the love of God, build strong families, and grow disciples of Jesus Christ.

Founded on the principles of the African Inland Church denomination, we welcome people from all walks of life to experience the transforming power of God's love in a warm, family-friendly environment.
